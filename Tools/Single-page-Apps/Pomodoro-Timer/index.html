Steps:
  • Decide on the task to be worked (click button for Task Statement Example)
  • Set the Pomodoro timer (typically for 25 minutes)
  • Work on the task
  • Stop when timer rings: take a short break (typically 5–10 minutes)
  • If three pomodoros, or less, have been worked: go back to Step 2
  • After the fourth pomodoro, take a long break (typically 20 to 30 minutes)
  • Once the long break is finished:
    • Go to step 1 (if work is not productive)
    • Or step 2 until work is done.
